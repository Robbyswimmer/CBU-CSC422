## Detailed Scoring Rubric

## Academic Integrity & Research Ethics

### Research Integrity Requirements
- All work must represent original research or substantial innovation
- Proper attribution of all sources, data, and collaborative work
- Ethical data use following appropriate guidelines and permissions
- Transparent reporting of methodology, limitations, and potential biases

### Collaboration Policies
- Individual research project with optional approved collaboration
- Any collaboration must be explicitly documented and pre-approved
- Shared resources (datasets, computing) must be properly attributed
- Peer consultation is encouraged but final work must be individual
